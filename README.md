<div align="center">

I work in the space where hardware design becomes real — writing RTL that synthesizes cleanly, building embedded systems that respond in microseconds, and thinking carefully about what happens at the register level. My background is in Electronics and Computer Engineering, and I am currently deepening that through an M.Tech in VLSI Design at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore.

<br/>

The work here reflects an engineering practice in progress: FPGA architectures in Verilog, microcontroller-based systems in Embedded C, and hardware experiments that prioritize correctness and physical behavior over abstraction. I am drawn to applications where hardware-level thinking changes what is possible — precision agriculture, renewable energy sensing, real-time control systems. I try to build things that are honest about what they are, and I care about getting the details right.
